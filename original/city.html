Your City

Ibirapuera Park is a sprawling urban park in São Paulo, known for its lush green spaces, tranquil lakes, and striking modernist architecture. The park is a hub for cultural activities and outdoor recreation, featuring museums, art installations, and walking trails. Visitors can explore the iconic Oscar Niemeyer-designed buildings, attend open-air concerts, or simply relax amidst the natural beauty of this urban oasis.

The São Paulo Museum of Art, commonly referred to as MASP, is a renowned cultural institution showcasing an impressive collection of European and Brazilian art. The museum is distinguished by its striking modernist architecture, designed by Lina Bo Bardi, and its innovative approach to displaying artwork on transparent easels. With works by renowned artists such as Van Gogh, Picasso, and Portinari, MASP offers an enriching experience for art enthusiasts and history lovers alike.

The Municipal Market of São Paulo, affectionately known as Mercadão, is a vibrant marketplace that delights visitors with its bustling atmosphere and diverse culinary offerings. Here, one can find an array of fresh produce, exotic fruits, gourmet cheeses, and traditional Brazilian delicacies. The market's iconic stained glass windows and ornate architecture add to its charm as a must-visit destination for food enthusiasts seeking authentic flavors and local gastronomic experiences.
